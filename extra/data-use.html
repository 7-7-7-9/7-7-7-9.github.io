How We Use Data

We collect data to improve your gaming experience and to understand how our website is performing. This data is used in two primary ways:

1. Saving Game Data

When you play games on our site, your progress and preferences are saved so that you can pick up where you left off. This includes:

  • In-game progress
  • High scores

2. Website Analytics

We also track website performance and user behavior using analytics tools. This data helps us understand:

  • How many users visit the website
  • Website Activity
  • Game Popularity
